Past Events

Here are some of our past events at LNU AI Society.

November 2025 • NEURAWAVE HACKATHON

Hackathon: Neurawave & Kalmar Science Park

We were honored to join Neurawave at Kalmar Science Park on November 21 for a fast-paced hackathon dedicated to tackling challenges in migraine relief. Students collaborated in teams to develop innovative business ideas, prototypes, designs, and AI/ML or Generative AI–based solutions.
The session highlighted practical applications of AI/ML in product development, along with valuable lessons on resilience and adaptability in building impactful solutions.

Neurawave and other companies based in Kalmar Science Park also shared opportunities for internships and bachelor's thesis projects, giving attendees a valuable chance to build industry connections.

November 2025 • COMPANY VISIT

Company Visit: Spiris | Visma

On November 25, we had the pleasure of visiting Spiris | Visma for an evening focused on applied AI in software development. The session began with pizza and an informal meet-and-greet before diving into practical demonstrations of how engineers build and deploy AI agents.
Attendees gained insight into real-world workflows, tools, and development practices used within the company, offering a clear view of how AI is integrated into modern software systems.

The Head of Technology also shared valuable guidance on the skills and mindsets they look for in emerging talent, providing students with direction and inspiration for their career paths.
